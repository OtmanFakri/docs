## Project Genesis

HealthStaff was conceived by Otman Fakri in response to observed inefficiencies in healthcare HR management. The project aims to address several key issues:

<Steps>
    <Step title="Inefficient Data Management">
        Excel-based systems were proving inadequate for handling complex HR data.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Security Concerns">
        Traditional methods posed risks to sensitive employee and medical information.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Limited Reporting Capabilities">
        Existing systems lacked advanced analytics and reporting features.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Scalability Issues">
        Growing healthcare organizations needed more robust, scalable solutions.
    </Step>
</Steps>

## Project Vision

<Note>
    HealthStaff envisions a future where healthcare HR management is seamless, secure, and data-driven, enabling organizations to focus more on patient care and less on administrative challenges.
</Note>

The project aims to:

- Streamline HR processes specific to healthcare settings
- Enhance data security and compliance with healthcare regulations
- Provide powerful analytics for informed decision-making
- Improve overall efficiency in healthcare HR departments
